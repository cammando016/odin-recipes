Crumbed Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breast fillets
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Flour
  • Eggs

Preparation

  1. Slice chicken in medium strips
  2. In 3 separate bowls, add the breadcrumbs, eggs and flour
  3. Dip each chicken slice into flour, then eggs, then breadcrumbs.
  4. Fry chicken strips on high heat and serve while hot
