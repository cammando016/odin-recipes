Potato Meal

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 chicken breast fillet
  • 100g diced bacon
  • Shredded cheese
  • Seasoning:
    • salt
    • pepper
    • paprika
    • rosemary
    • cumin

Preparation

  1. Slice potatoes into small cubes
  2. Let sit in cold water for 2 minutes
  3. Drain water and season with salt, pepper and paprika
  4. Spary with cooking oil and cook in air fryer for 18 minutes at 190 degrees
  5. Butterfly cut the chicken breast to get 2 thinner fillets
  6. Generously season both sides of each fillet with all seasonings
  7. Heat oil in frying pan
  8. Cook chicken breasts on medium heat flipping regularly to prevent burning
  9. Once cooked, allow to cool then using 2 forks pull chicken into thin strips
  10. Fry diced bacon until crispy
  11. Once pototoes are finished, place on a tray with baking paper, then top with chicken, bacon and mix together
  12. Top with shredded cheese and place in oven at 200 degrees until cheese melts
