Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 jar pasta sauce
  • Handful of spaghetti
  • Seasoning:
    • salt
    • pepper
    • garlic powder
    • onion powder
    • chilli flakes

Preparation

  1. Put water on to boil with generous amount of salt in water
  2. Heat oil on frying pan
  3. Place meat onto pan, break up and cook on high heat until browned
  4. Once water boils, add spaghetti to pot
  5. While spaghetti cooks, pour pasta sauce jar into frying pan with mince
  6. Reduce heat, let sauce simmer while stirring occasionally
  7. Once pasta is cooked al dente, drain water and add into sauce and mince mix
  8. Mix together and serve
